About the Club

Who We Are
The Mountain House E-Sports Club is a group of casual and competitive players alike determined to pursue and enjoy gaming. Formed in 2021, the club has more than 120 active members.

What We Do As the Mountain House E-Sports team, we serve as a platform for gamers to express and develop their interest in gaming and it's culture. We host tournaments ranging from Valorant to Super Smash Bros Ultimate, with emphasis placed on games that members want to compete in. The club also hosts Game Nights, in which members and interested students can visit, meet new people and play games from Fifa to League of Legends.
