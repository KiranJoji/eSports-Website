The MHHS eSports Team is a team of our most skilled individuals, competing against various teams across the map to distinguish themselves as the best.


They compete through PlayVS, an organization that acts as a middleman between MHHS and the competitions.

They are managed by our amazing advisor, Ms. Wone. She ensures that the whole process is seamless and that our teams have the best possible chance of victory.
