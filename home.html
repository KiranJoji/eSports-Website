Who We Are

MHHS eSports consists of two parts; the team and the club.

The team is the competitive side, working hard to improve and compete against other skilled teams.

The club is the casual side, for those who enjoy games, but do not wish to compete at high levels.

Our Mission is to create a space for MHHS students to express themselves , as well as to forge new bonds through the games that they play.

Join Us!

We are a massive club with over 100 members, but we always have space for more! The process of joining is also quite easy!

If you wish to join the club, all you have to do is email our club president, Rohan Potu, at 1074499@lammersvilleusd.net.

To join a team for a certain game, you must wait until tryouts begin. If you wish to know when the tryouts will occur, please email our advisor, Mrs. Wone, at kwone@lammersvilleusd.net
